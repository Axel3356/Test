Frequently Asked Questions

How much does moving help cost in Zurich?

The rate is 100 CHF per hour, all-inclusive. No extra charges for stairs, fuel, or parking.

What areas do you cover?

Zurich city, the greater Zurich area (Kanton Zürich), and Aargau including Aarau. Longer-distance moves to other Swiss cantons are possible — just ask.

Can you move a sofa or a bed?

Yes! The SUV cargo space is 190 cm long, 110 cm wide, and 80 cm high — fits most sofas, single and double beds, wardrobes, and large boxes. Tip: Many modern mattresses can be folded in half, which saves a lot of space. For bed frames, it helps to disassemble them beforehand (legs off, slats bundled) so everything fits more efficiently and we can get it all done in fewer trips.

Is the service pet-friendly?

Absolutely. Dogs and cats are welcome to travel in the vehicle during the move at no extra cost.

Do you speak English?

Yes — fully fluent in English. As an expat myself, I understand the challenges of settling in Switzerland and offer a relaxed, English-speaking experience.

Do you help with IKEA pickups and deliveries?

Yes, I regularly help with IKEA pickups, flat-pack furniture deliveries, and apartment relocations across Zurich and Aargau.

Do you help with loading and unloading?

Yes — I help carry, load, and unload your items. No extra charge for stairs or elevators.

Is there a minimum booking time?

No strict minimum, but most jobs take at least one hour. You only pay for the actual time used.

Can you move between cantons, e.g. Zurich to Basel or Bern?

Yes, longer-distance moves between Swiss cantons are possible. Contact me with your route and I'll confirm availability and pricing.

Can I ride along during the move?

Yes, you are welcome to ride along. It's your move — I'm just here to make it easier.

How far in advance do I need to book?

As soon as possible is always better, but I can often accommodate last-minute requests. Just send me a WhatsApp or call to check.
